The Flagship Work

A story the world has been waiting for.

Bihar, British Colonial India · The Fatel Rozack · Trinidad

Volume I: The Epic follows Shakuntala — a Hindu woman of extraordinary grit, whose faith is forged in the plains of Bihar and tested by the raw cruelty of British colonial rule. Recruited into Indian indentureship and bound for a new world, she boards the Fatel Rozack with everything she is and nothing she was promised.

This is not just historical fiction. It is a reckoning — with colonialism, with identity, with the indomitable will of a woman who refuses to be erased.

A multi-volume series. Volume I available now.
