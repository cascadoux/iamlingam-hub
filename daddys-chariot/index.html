Our Mission
No Father Left Behind
Distance should never be a barrier between a father and his child. We provide the chariot so fathers can show up — every court date, every visitation, every milestone.

Transportation Services

Safe, reliable rides to court appearances, visitation appointments, and family meetings — at no cost to the father.

Father Advocacy

We stand beside fathers in the systems that too often work against them — navigating bureaucracy so they can focus on their families.

Community Support

Connecting fathers with peer networks, mentors, and resources that strengthen their role in their children's lives.

Why We Exist
The Gap We're Closing

Thousands of fathers lose contact with their children not because they don't care — but because they can't get there. A missed court date, a failed visitation, a system that doesn't account for the father without a car.

Daddy's Chariot Inc was founded to eliminate that gap. One ride at a time. One relationship restored at a time.

What We Do

Our Programs

Every program is built around one belief: a present father changes everything.

Visitation Rides

Scheduled transportation to child visitation appointments, ensuring fathers never miss court-ordered time with their children due to lack of transport.

Transportation

Court Date Escort

Professional, on-time transportation to family court, custody hearings, and child welfare appointments — with confirmation reminders sent 24 hours before.

Legal Support

Chariot Connect

Volunteer driver network matching fathers with community members willing to provide rides in their area. Building community, one connection at a time.

Community

Emergency Transport Fund

Uber and Lyft credits for fathers facing urgent transportation needs — a hospital visit, an emergency custody hearing, a child's first day of school.

Emergency Aid

Father's License Program

Assistance obtaining or reinstoring a driver's license — helping fathers become independent and reliable in their children's lives for the long term.

Independence

School & Activity Rides

Getting fathers to school events, sports games, recitals, and milestone moments — because presence in the small moments matters just as much.

Family Life
